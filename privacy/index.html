HPASS Privacy Policy

Updated July 31, 2023

© HPASS is a tool to generate secure and reproducible passwords. Passwords are generated locally, on demand, and are not stored on any remote server or locally. HPASS does not request, collect, store, or transfer any personal information and does not facilitate any communication between users.

HPASS directly does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies, however Google AdSense is used to display advertisements on HPASS website and mobile app. In the feature other similar methods and services could be used to serve ads as well.

AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to serve personalized ads based on your interests and browsing behavior. These cookies allow Google and its partners to display ads that are most relevant to you.

Opt-out: If you do not wish to see personalized ads, you can opt-out of Google's use of cookies for ad personalization by visiting Google's Ads Settings page Please note that even if you opt-out of personalized ads, you may still see non-personalized ads.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at support@hpass.app

Thank you for using HPASS!
