HPASS Privacy Policy

HPASS is a tool to generate secure and reproducible passwords. It does not collect, store, or transfer any user data (names, emails, etc.) and does not facilitate any communication between users.

The following three data items (Settings) are stored for the user convenience on the local device:

  1. Secret - this is user provided (or app suggested and user approved) string. The purpose of this string is to customize password for the user.
  2. Special Character(s) - one (or more) special character(s) to be embeded in the generated password to satisfy typical websites requrements.
  3. Length - desired password length

HPASS directly does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies, however Google AdSense is used to display advertisements on HPASS website and mobile app.

AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to serve personalized ads based on your interests and browsing behavior. These cookies allow Google and its partners to display ads that are most relevant to you.

Opt-out: If you do not wish to see personalized ads, you can opt-out of Google's use of cookies for ad personalization by visiting Google's Ads Settings page Please note that even if you opt-out of personalized ads, you may still see non-personalized ads.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at support@hpass.app

Thank you for using HPASS!
