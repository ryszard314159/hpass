HPASS Privacy Policy

Updated July 31, 2023

© HPASS is a tool to generate secure and reproducible passwords. Passwords are generated locally, on demand, and are not stored on any remote server or locally. HPASS does not request, collect, store, or transfer personal information or facilitate user communication.

HPASS does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies directly. However, it uses Google AdSense to display advertisements on the HPASS website and mobile app. It might also use similar methods and services to serve ads in the future.

AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to serve personalized ads based on your interests and browsing behavior. These cookies allow Google and its partners to display ads most relevant to you.

Opt-out: If you do not wish to see personalized ads, you can opt out of Google's use of cookies for ad personalization by visiting Google's Ads Settings page. Please note that even if you opt out of personalized ads, you may still see non-personalized ads.

If you have any questions or concerns, please get in touch with support@hpass.app

Thank you for using HPASS!
